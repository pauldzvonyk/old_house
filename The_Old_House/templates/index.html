A little bit about us.

The Old House is a new modern bar and restaurant recently opened in the heart of Arklow, we serve Lunch, Dinner, Brunch & Drinks.

  • Parking Available, Free off-street parking, Television, Highchairs Available, Wheelchair Accessible.
  • We Serve Alcohol, Full Bar, Wine and Beer.
  • z Have no cash? - No Worry, We Will Gladly Accept Your Credit Card Payments.

We offer a wide range of cuisine from Irish to oriental while also offering a vast variety of beverages. Our classic style restaurant upstairs offers two and three course meals form 6pm till 9.30pm tues-fri. Our downstairs bar serves bar food daily from 10.30am till 9.30pm.Live music every Saturday from 10.30pm.
